BMW

BMW is primarily owned by the Quandt family, with Stefan Quandt holding 27.02% of the company and Susanne Klatten owning 21.9%. The remaining shares are publicly traded and held by institutional investors, mutual funds, and other individual investors1. BMW, officially known as Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, has been a powerhouse in luxury automotive manufacturing since its founding in 1916.

The BMW X6 M Competition is a high-performance luxury SUV that combines aggressive styling with 617 horsepower from a 4.4-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo V-8 engine. It features 48V mild-hybrid technology, M xDrive all-wheel drive, and an 8-speed M Sport Automatic Transmission for extreme performance. Inside, it boasts a BMW Curved Display, M Multi-Functional Seats, and ambient lighting, creating a premium driving experience. The exterior is defined by M-specific design elements, including quad exhausts, a rear diffuser, and a high-gloss black M kidney grille.
